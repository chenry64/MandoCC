Pentatonic Scale

For a pentatonic scale, remove the 4th and 7th notes. The G Major Pentatonic scale is as follows:

  • G (root)
  • A (major 2nd)
  • B (major 3rd)
  • D (perfect 5th)
  • E (major 6th)
